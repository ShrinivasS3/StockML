Attributes(the name of each column):
Close - 
The value at which the stock closed that day.
Volume - 
The number of shares that were traded that day or the amount of shares that exchanged hands.
EMA 5 - 
The Exponential Moving Average (EMA) with a period of 5.
EMA 13 - 
The EMA with a period of 13.
EMA 26 - 
The EMA with a period of 26.
MACD - 
The Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) indicator that shows the relationship between two moving averages of a security's price.
Signal - 
The 9-day EMA of the MACD.
RSI - 
The Relative Strength Index (RSI) used by traders to assess the price momentum of a stock or other security.
ROC - 
The Rate-of-Change (ROC) indicator, also referred to as simply Momentum, that measures the percent change in price from one period to the next.
Worked? - 
Whether good returns would have been made within 7 days of buying the stock that day. 1 = Yes, 0 = No.
Percentage Returns - 
The maximum returns the stock gave within 7 days (7 trading days) of buying it.
